HATSUNE MIKU: Colorful stage!

It's me again! Now, I'll tell you about my favortie game! That game is HATSUNE MIKU: Colorful stage, a.k.a Project sekai.

What is Colorful stage?

Choose the members of your own squad and play through "Live Show" on five different difficulty settings! To get the best score, tap, hold, and flick your way to the finish.
You may also level up your characters and unlock outfits by earning XP and other things.

How do you play it?

  1. Choose any song to play.
  2. Adjust timing and speed to your liking
  3. Once you see notes, tap on them!
  4. Swipe up on the notes with arrows on them.
  5. Long press the elongated notes and let go once they're done!
  6. Try your best to not get any "bad" or "miss" since these will take away from your health.
  7. Once your healthbar reaches zero, restart and try again.

Why do I play it?

  • The amount of songs to play keeps me entertained for a long time.
  • The Co-op mode or the multiplayer lets me play with other fellow players!
  • The storyline of each character's backstories are intriguing and well written.
  • The designs of the character are well made and pretty
